\section*{2}

Suppose that $G$ is a finite group with 60 elements. What are the orders of possible subgroups of $G$?

\vspace{\baselineskip}

The possible orders of subgroups of $G$ are the divisors of $G$: 1,2,3,4,5,6,10,12,15,20,30,60.
